The Pantheon

Each deity commands its own domain — install one, install all

𓂀

Anubis — Judge of the Dead

pantheon weigh · pantheon judge

The jackal god who weighs your system's heart. Scans 7 domains and 58+ rules for infrastructure waste — cache directories, orphaned build artifacts, stale model weights. Then renders judgment: purge what's dead.

𓂓

Ka — Spirit of the Dead

pantheon ka

The life force that persists after death. Ka hunts ghost apps — the spirits of uninstalled software that linger as .plist files, Launch Services phantoms, and orphaned cache directories. Sub-second parallel scan.

𓉡

Hathor — Goddess of Reflection

pantheon mirror

The mirror goddess finds your duplicates. Three-phase matching: size grouping → partial hash (first+last 4KB) → full SHA-256. Smart keep/delete recommendations with protected paths. Web UI included.

𓁵

Sekhmet — The Warrior Guardian

pantheon guard

Ra's lioness enforcer and destroyer of threats. Sekhmet audits memory pressure, classifies processes across 5 types, and surgically terminates zombie and orphan processes consuming your resources.

𓆄

Ma'at — Truth and Order

pantheon maat · pantheon scales

The goddess of cosmic order wields her feather and her scales. Quality governance with coverage enforcement, canon linkage verification, and policy-as-code. The Feather Weight score gamifies truth.

𓁹

Horus — The All-Seeing Eye

pantheon sight

The redeemer who restores what can be saved. Horus heals Spotlight and Launch Services, fixing phantom registrations and ghost entries. His shared filesystem index lets every deity see everything — walk once, query forever.

𓆣

Khepri — Scarab of Renewal

pantheon scarab

The dawn beetle who transforms and renews. Khepri discovers and audits hosts across your network — Docker container inspection, ARP scanning, port discovery. Fleet-scale infrastructure hygiene.

𓇼

Seba — The Star Map

pantheon seba

The Egyptian word for "star." Seba visualizes your infrastructure as an interactive network graph — mapping the constellation of services, hosts, and dependencies across your environment.

𓁟

Thoth — God of Knowledge

pantheon mcp · pantheon install-brain

The ibis-headed scribe who records all things. Persistent AI memory with 98% context reduction. Neural file classification via the Brain module. MCP server for Claude, Cursor, and Windsurf.

𓁶

Hapi — God of the Flood

pantheon hapi

The Nile god who controls the flow of abundance. Hapi detects hardware resources — GPUs, Neural Engines, VRAM — and optimizes resource allocation so nothing runs dry.

☀️

Ra — The Supreme Overseer

planned

The sun god who rules all deities. Ra is the autonomous hypervisor — orchestrating every deity, monitoring system health, and making autonomous decisions about when to scan, judge, guard, and heal.
